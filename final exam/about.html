About us

"KazHisO" is a dynamic initiative led by enthusiastic Kazakh historians, offering an interactive platform to explore the nation's rich heritage through maps, articles, and videos. Committed to accessibility, the company employs innovative tools like webinars and online courses to engage a diverse audience in discovering and appreciating Kazakhstan's cultural legacy.

Our Mission

At KazHisO, our mission is to illuminate the diverse and dynamic history of Kazakhstan. We are dedicated to providing a comprehensive and accessible platform that uncovers the layers of the nation's past, bridging historical narratives with contemporary realities. Through curated content and engaging resources, we inspire curiosity, foster a deeper appreciation for Kazakhstan's cultural heritage, and strive to be a trusted guide on the journey through the rich tapestry of its history.

Our Story

In 2020, a group of enthusiasts from Kazakhstan launched "KazHisO," a web platform dedicated to studying and promoting the country's history. The project aimed to make knowledge about Kazakhstan's history accessible and engaging to a wide audience. The development team focused on creating a user-friendly design with interactive features. The site includes a map of historical events, and historians crafted compelling articles, videos, and interviews. Using social media, the company conducts webinars, contests, and online courses, attracting audiences of all ages.
